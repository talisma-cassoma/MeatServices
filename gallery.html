Safe, inclusive, and engaged communities are not born. They are deliberately made and maintained by invested community members and passionate Trust & Safety professionals. Protect Advertisers

The experience that people have on a platform impacts brand perception. This is true not only of the platform’s reputation but also for the brands who appear in ads within the platform. Because consumers may view ads placed next to negative content as an intentional endorsement, content moderation is critical to protect advertisers. Research shows that purchase intent is significantly stifled and consumers are less likely to associate with a brand when it is displayed next to unsafe or brand-adverse content.
